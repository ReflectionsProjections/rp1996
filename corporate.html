What's in it for the Corporate Participants

The Midwestern ACM Chapters Conference would not be possible without the support of our corporate friends. We use their financial support to help keep participant admissions costs as low as possible.
  • The official invitation
  • The online registration form

Conference Corporate Sponsors donating $200 or more will receive the following in exchange for their support of the conference:

  • Full conference access for all corporate participants
  • A booth in the Computing Employment Fair on October 11, 1996
  • Acknowledgement both online and in printed conference material
  • optional interview rooms and ethernet access
A corporation can become an Official Sponsor of the conference by donating in excess of $500 and will receive:
  • Priority Placement in Employment Fair
  • Top Billing on WWW and flyers
  • Preference in interview room assignment
For an example of last year's online corporate publicity, see the Conference '95 Sponsors Page
